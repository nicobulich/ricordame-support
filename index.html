Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I create a task?
    Tap the add button and type your task title.
  • How do categories work?
    Create categories and assign colors to organize tasks visually.
  • What happens when I complete a task?
    Completed tasks are moved to the Archive and remain accessible.
  • Does RicordaMe require an account?
    No. RicordaMe works without any login.
  • Where is my data stored?
    Your data is stored locally on your device.
